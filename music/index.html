  • Gregory's March

From their Spotify:

Tattered Rabbit is best known for their psychedelic walls of sound consisting of whirling effects, dripping harmonies and haunting melodies. It is easy to drift into their dense realm of sonic contrast that combines 60's Psych Rock with a more contemporary yet timeless and organic form. Much of the lyrical content tells the story of our mortal struggles from a foreign yet familiar point of view. Their music is often described as spooky and a little circusy. Flurrying chromaticism paired with their rich coloring of tone, brings about a sense of endless motion while maintaining a dark yet beautiful landscape. It is easy to hear creative influence from bands such as The Doors, King Crimson and Pink Floyd.
